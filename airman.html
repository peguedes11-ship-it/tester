AIR MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Get right in his face when the tornadoes are spreading out. It's often easier to tank a hit and spam your blaster.

  • The Tornado Wall: He blows tornadoes that block your shots. You have to weave through them or jump over them to hit his body.

  • The Wind Pushes You: His fan pushes Mega Man backward. You will need to constantly hold forward on the D-Pad to maintain your ground.

  • Weakness: The LEAF SHIELD (Wood Man) makes quick work of him. Just activate it and throw it directly into his chest.
  • Pattern: He shoots formations of tornadoes three times, then jumps across to the other side of the screen.

  • The Meme: Despite the famous song "I Cannot Defeat Airman," he is actually considered one of the easiest bosses if you just play aggressively and spam your attack.

  • Stage Goblins: In his sky stage, enemies hide in the clouds. Shoot them right as their horns appear before they jump out.

  • Stage Tips: The tricky jumps across the floating platforms require you to memorize when the platforms spawn. Patience is key!
