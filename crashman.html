CRASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Crash Man's movements are reactive. He jumps when you shoot! Use this to manipulate him.

  • Bait the Jump: Shoot to make him jump, run under him, and shoot him in the back when he lands.

  • Avoid the Bombs: While in the air, he drops a sticky Crash Bomber that explodes after a few seconds. Do not stand near it!

  • Weakness: The AIR SHOOTER (Air Man) makes this fight trivial. Because Air Shooter goes upwards, just shoot it when he jumps, and it hits him perfectly.
  • Pattern: He walks forward. If you attack, he immediately jumps high and drops an explosive down to where you are.

  • Patience: Don't panic fire. Only shoot when you are prepared to dodge his incoming drop bomb.

  • The Vertical Stage: His stage involves climbing massive ladders. Moving platforms ride along rails; just hop on and ride them up.

  • Stage Tips: The Pipis (bird robots) drop eggs that spawn mini-birds. Use the Leaf Shield to protect yourself while climbing the ladders to avoid being knocked off.
