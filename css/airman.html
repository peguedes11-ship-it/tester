WOOD MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: The only time you can deal damage is immediately after he throws his Leaf Shield at you.

  • Jumping the Shield: When he throws his shield, jump perfectly straight up or slightly forward to clear it without taking massive damage.

  • Watch the Ceiling: While his shield is up, leaves will fall from the top of the screen. Stand in the safe gaps to avoid getting hit.

  • Weakness: ATOMIC FIRE (Heat Man) is his ultimate weakness. A fully charged Atomic Fire blast can wipe out almost his entire health bar in one hit!
  • Pattern: He creates a shield, summons falling leaves, throws his shield forward, jumps, and repeats.

  • Alternative Weapon: If you don't have Atomic Fire, the Metal Blade also works exceptionally well against him.

  • Robo-Apes: His stage features giant robotic gorillas. The safest way to kill them is to stay at a distance and shoot, or simply jump over them if you are fast enough.

  • Stage Tips: Watch out for the robotic dogs that breathe fire. Slide under their fire arcs or use the Metal Blade to dispatch them from an angle.
