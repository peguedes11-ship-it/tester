QUICK MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: He is incredibly fast, so focus on evasion first and only attack when he is at the peak of his jumps.

  • Dodge the Boomerangs: Quick Man throws boomerangs that curve back to him. Stay near the center of the room so you have space to dodge.

  • Weakness 1: The TIME STOPPER (Flash Man) is his main weakness. Using it will drain half of his health bar without you doing anything.

  • Weakness 2: After using Time Stopper, switch to your basic Mega Buster to finish off his remaining health.
  • Pattern: He runs at extreme speeds, leaps high into the air, and tosses out three boomerangs before charging again.

  • The Lasers: His stage is infamous for the instant-kill Force Beams. You must memorize the drop paths or use the Time Stopper to freeze the beams.

  • Jump Strategy: Keep your jumps short when he runs at you. Overcommitting to a jump will likely cause you to land right on his boomerangs.

  • Stage Tips: In the dark sections of his stage, enemies known as Scworms will spawn infinitely. Keep moving down the ladders quickly to avoid taking unnecessary damage.
