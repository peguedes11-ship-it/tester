HEAT MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Shoot him when he throws his fire out, but immediately prepare to jump when he transforms.

  • Dodge the Fireball: Once you shoot him, he turns into a flaming comet and charges straight across the room. You MUST jump over this.

  • Don't Spam: If you spam the buster, he will repeatedly turn into a comet and you won't have time to dodge. Take it one shot at a time.

  • Weakness: BUBBLE LEAD (Bubble Man) destroys him. It acts like water putting out a fire, dealing heavy damage.
  • Pattern: He throws three pillars of fire, waits for you to hit him, and then charges at you.

  • Yoku Blocks: His stage contains the most infamous disappearing block puzzle in video game history over a pit of lava.

  • Skip the Blocks: Use "Item-2" (the flying jet sled you get from Air Man) to completely fly over the disappearing blocks and save yourself the headache!

  • Stage Tips: Watch out for the Sniper Joes in the mech suits at the beginning. Wait for them to jump, slide under, and avoid taking unnecessary hits.
