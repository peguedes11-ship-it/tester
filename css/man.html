FLASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Since the room has staggered terrain, hit him when he runs onto the same platform height as you.

  • Time Stopper: Flash Man will freeze time. When he does, he unleashes a rapid-fire buster spread. Jump to avoid being frozen on the same level as him.

  • Room Layout: Use the jagged terrain to your advantage. If he is lower than you, you can shoot over him or jump away easily.

  • Weakness: The METAL BLADE shreds him. Since you can aim in 8 directions, you can hit him no matter what level of the floor he is running on.
  • Pattern: He paces back and forth quickly, uses his Time Stopper to freeze you, fires a spread of shots, and repeats.

  • Crash Bomber Weakness: He is technically also highly weak to Crash Man's bombs, but landing them with his fast speed can be trickier than using the Metal Blade.

  • Slippery Stage: Almost the entire stage is made of ice. Try not to change direction abruptly, or you'll slide into enemies.

  • Stage Tips: The stage is a labyrinth. To get the 'E-Tanks' and 'Item-1' power-ups, you'll need the Crash Bomber to break down the walls blocking your path.
