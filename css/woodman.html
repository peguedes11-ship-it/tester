BUBBLE MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Hit him while he is descending from the ceiling, right before he shoots his bubbles.

  • Watch the Spikes: The ceiling of his boss room is lined with instant-kill spikes. Because of the underwater physics, you jump higher. Do NOT jump too high!

  • Avoid the Bouncing Bubbles: He shoots bubbles that bounce along the floor. Stand in the gaps between their bounces.

  • Weakness: The METAL BLADE is highly effective against him. Since you can aim the blade in 8 directions, you can easily hit him while he swims near the ceiling.
  • Pattern: He swims to the top of the room, drops down, fires his arm cannon, and then shoots bouncing bubbles before swimming back up.

  • Water Physics: Take advantage of your increased jump height in his stage, but remember that falling takes longer, which requires better timing.

  • Frog Enemies: In the stage, the giant robotic frogs release smaller frogs. Shoot the large frogs quickly before the screen gets overwhelmed.

  • Stage Tips: The falling waterfalls push you down if you try to jump through them. Compensate for the drag when making jumps over bottomless pits.
