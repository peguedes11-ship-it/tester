METAL MAN

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Shoot him while he is walking, but be ready to jump when he stops to throw blades.

  • Manage the Conveyor Belt: The fight is much easier when the conveyor belt pulls you away from him, rather than to him.

  • The Jump Trick: If you approach him closely, he will jump over you. You can use this to your advantage to stay on the side of the room where the conveyor belt is working in your favor.

  • Weakness: The METAL BLADE kills him in very few hits. It's his own weapon, so you'll only be able to use this later in the game.
  • Pattern: He typically throws two or three blades, jumps, and then repeats

  • Start with Metal Man: He is widely considered the best first boss to fight because his weapon is extremely useful for the rest of the game.

  • Jump Strategy: Jump over his blades, not at him, to avoid going getting hit, especially when he tosses three in a row.

  • Stage Tips: The hardest part about Metal Man's stage are the two big jumps from elevated platforms across a gap. Make sure to get to the edge of the platform before jumping. Make sure you're running before you jump.
